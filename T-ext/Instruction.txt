Follow These Steps To add Thos Extensionn to your Chrome browser.

1.Open Chrome
2.Type "chrome://extensions/" in the url without the quotations
3.Check developer options
4.Click Load unpacked extension
5.Browse and select the Extension folder in your downloads
6.After it has been succesfully intergrated in your browser, you just have to select a text and click on the action icon in the browser(top right).
